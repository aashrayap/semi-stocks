# Lumentum -- Q2 FY2026 Earnings

This was a real optical acceleration quarter, not just a narrative quarter. Revenue grew 65.5% year over year, margins expanded sharply, backlog built, and management disclosed both OCS and CPO demand stepping up.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Q2 FY2026 | Q1 FY2026 | Q2 FY2025 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue | $665.5M | $533.8M | $402.2M |
| GAAP gross margin | 36.1% | 34.0% | 24.8% |
| Non-GAAP gross margin | 42.5% | 39.4% | 32.3% |
| GAAP operating margin | 9.7% | 1.3% | -12.8% |
| Non-GAAP operating margin | 25.2% | 18.7% | 7.9% |
| GAAP EPS | $0.89 | $0.05 | -$0.88 |
| Non-GAAP EPS | $1.67 | $1.10 | $0.42 |
| Cash + short-term investments | $1.155B | $1.122B | — |

Revenue mix in Q2 FY2026 was $443.7M of components and $221.8M of systems.

## Guidance

- Q3 FY2026 revenue: $780M to $830M
- Q3 FY2026 non-GAAP operating margin: 30.0% to 31.0%
- Q3 FY2026 non-GAAP EPS: $2.15 to $2.35

## Key Management Quotes

- **CEO Michael Hurlston:** revenue grew more than 65% year over year and non-GAAP operating margin expanded by more than 1,700 basis points.
- **CEO Michael Hurlston:** OCS demand is extraordinary and has already pushed backlog well beyond $400M.
- **CEO Michael Hurlston:** Lumentum received an incremental multi-hundred-million-dollar CPO order deliverable in the first half of calendar 2027.
