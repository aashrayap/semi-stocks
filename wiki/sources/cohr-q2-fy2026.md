# Coherent -- Q2 FY2026 Earnings

Another clean quarter for the consensus optical name. Revenue, margins, and EPS all grew year over year, driven by datacenter and communications demand, and management kept guiding strong growth with ongoing capacity expansion.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Q2 FY2026 | Q1 FY2026 | Q2 FY2025 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue | $1.686B | $1.581B | $1.435B |
| Revenue growth | +17.5% YoY | — | — |
| GAAP gross margin | 36.9% | 36.6% | 35.5% |
| Non-GAAP gross margin | 39.0% | 38.7% | 38.2% |
| GAAP EPS | $0.76 | $1.19 | $0.44 |
| Non-GAAP EPS | $1.29 | $1.16 | $0.95 |
| GAAP net income | $147M | $226M | $103M |
| Non-GAAP net income | $248M | $221M | $185M |

## Guidance

- Q3 FY2026 revenue: $1.70B to $1.84B
- Q3 FY2026 non-GAAP gross margin: 38.5% to 40.5%
- Q3 FY2026 non-GAAP operating expenses: $320M to $340M
- Q3 FY2026 non-GAAP tax rate: 18% to 20%
- Q3 FY2026 non-GAAP EPS: $1.28 to $1.48

## Key Management Quotes

- **CEO Jim Anderson:** strong year-over-year growth was driven by another quarter of datacenter and communications demand.
- **CEO Jim Anderson:** Coherent expects continued strong growth in the second half of FY2026 and throughout FY2027 as datacenter and communications demand stays strong and production capacity expands.
- **CFO Sherri Luther:** revenue growth and gross margin expansion drove the EPS improvement, and the company is continuing to ramp capital investment to support demand.
